Essential Facts About Energy Efficiency


Energy-efficient windows and doors are designed to maintain a comfortable indoor climate by keeping your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer. These high-quality, cost-effective solutions minimize air leakage, helping to sustain your desired temperature while reducing energy costs. When it comes to windows, RGB Windows stands out as one of Canada’s leading window installation contractors.


25%

Did you know that up to 25% of a home’s total heat loss happens through its windows?

3

Triple-pane windows are the most energy-efficient models available on the market today.

60%

Heating makes up approximately 60% of the energy consumption in the average Canadian home.

96%

Low-E coated windows can reflect up to 96% of heat and light energy, enhancing energy efficiency and indoor comfort.
